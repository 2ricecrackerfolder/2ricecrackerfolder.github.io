Summer Mexico Outreach

RHCCC is partnered with Azusa Pacific University for an short-term mission trip in Ensenada, Mexico on July 23-29. We will be conducting two Vacation BIble Schools for the children at El Zorrillo and Praderas. In addition, we will be holding an outreach in Praderas. Registration is now full and closed. Please join us in prayer for the team as they prepare and are sent out to the mission field.
